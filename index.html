Your trip at a glance

South-to-north, matching your booked flights — into Rome, out of Venice. 🎂 Sep 14 — Murano, Burano & the birthday

Shape of the day: early train from Mestre to the island, islands through the afternoon, back ~5:30 PM, sunset + dinner, late train back. All independent, by waterbus.

Getting there & the ticket

1
Train Mestre → Venezia S. Lucia (12 min), then walk (~15 min) or Line 4.1/5.2 to Fondamente Nove — the island launch point.
2
Buy a 24-hour vaporetto pass (€25) — cheaper than ~4 singles. Validate at the reader every boarding.

① Murano — glassmaking (~2 hrs)

3
Line 12 (or 4.1) → Murano Colonna (~10–15 min).
4
Walk Fondamenta dei Vetrai for a live glassblowing demo (working furnace like Wave Murano Glass). Optional: Museo del Vetro, Basilica dei Santi Maria e Donato. Look for the "Vetro Artistico Murano" label when buying. Walk to Murano Faro.

② Burano — lacemaking (~2 hrs)

5
Line 12 from Murano Faro → Burano (~30–40 min via Mazzorbo).
6
Piazza Galuppi: the Museo del Merletto (Lace Museum), San Martino + its leaning tower, the rainbow houses, Buranelli cookies.

③ Return

7
Line 12 from Burano → Fondamente Nove (~45 min). Leave Burano by ~4:30–5 PM.
Route in one line: Fondamente Nove → Murano Colonna → (walk) Murano Faro → Burano → back to Fondamente Nove.

Birthday evening

  • Sunset (~7:15 PM): Skyline Rooftop Bar (Giudecca), a sunset gondola, or the Zattere / Accademia Bridge.
  • Dinner (~8 PM): La Zucca — canal-side balcony, creative vegetarian (reserve the canal table). Alternatives: Ai Mercanti (vegan menu), Le Spighe.
  • Late regional train back to Mestre (they run past midnight).
